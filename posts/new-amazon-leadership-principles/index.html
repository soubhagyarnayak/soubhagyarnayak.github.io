New Amazon Leadership Principles

2021-07-02
1 minute read

I am a big fan of Amazon leadership principles. Those principles help one to be disciplined and take right decisions. I do not work at Amazon. Still I revisit the leadership principles from time to time. I also reflect my own behavior against those.

Amazon has just added two more leadership principles. This has been done just before Jeff Bezos left Amazon. Jeff Bezos is the founder of Amazon and has shaped the company and its culture. Amazon has built a strong reputation for its customer obsession, efficiency and high standards. It is considered as one of the best technology companies. While Jeff Bezos and Amazon have successfully delighted the customers, there are a lot of criticism for many other cultures. A company is built to generate profit. To generate profit, it needs to give the best service to its customer at the least price. Amazon nails this perfectly. But every company needs employees to serve the customers. Platform centric companies like Amazon needs to care for their vendors and sellers as well. And Amazon has become very big and is at a position to make large impact to the world. The two new leadership principles tries to specify the guidelines for these aspects.

The two new Amazon leadership principles are

  1. Strive to be Earth’s Best Employer: It seems to address the criticism towards the treatment of employees at Amazon.
  2. Success and Scale Bring Broad Responsibility: It seems to address the criticism towards partners and employees.

With the course of time, we will see whether the new leadership principles are making some lasting impact or not. But a positive step towards that direction is good. This also highlights the point that the success of a company is not be measured in the amount of money it is making. The satisfaction of customers, employees, vendors and the positive impact made are better measures.
