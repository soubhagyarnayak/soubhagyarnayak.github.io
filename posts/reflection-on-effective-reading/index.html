Reflection on Effective Reading

2021-04-05
2 minute read

Reading is an important skill for everyone. Reading helps us to gather information, understand concepts and connect with others. If you are a knowledge worker, then you need to read fast. But reading fast alone won’t help. Gathering of knowledge must be followed with processing of the knowledge, retention of knowledge and utilisation of knowledge.

Understanding of content

Processing of knowledge will vary from reading material to reading material. One need not put a lot of effort while reading news. The content is factual and easy to consume. On the other hand, reading a technical book or article involving deep concept will take time to understand. Their many techniques one can use to handle rich content.

  1. We can read slowly and spend time understanding and reflecting. Here reading fast won’t help as reading without understanding the content won’t add value.
  2. We can do multiple rounds of reading. One common technique is to do a round of fast reading followed with multiple rounds of slow reading. In the fast reading step, we generally spend some time taking notes or highlighting important content.
  3. Reflecting on the reading also helps in processing of the content. One can either discuss the content with an audience, write an article on the content or simply reflect by thinking through the content.
  4. If it is a technical content, then one can spend time applying the content to understand it better. The same holds for mathematical content.

Retention of content

Retention of knowledge is hard. One of the common complaint among avid readers is that they forget things they have read. But reading without retention is fine only for the pleasure of reading. Without retaining the content, we won’t be able to apply the content. We can use a few techniques to improve our retention.

  1. If we reflect on the content after reading it, then it helps on retention. After you complete a book, go ahead and write a review. If you like an idea from the book, then write a blog post on this. Being part of a reading club or having a friend reading the book also helps as we discuss the content among us.
  2. Some content are easy to retain by applying. This is mostly relevant for technical and mathematical content.
  3. Spaced repetition is also a nice tool for retention. One can take note while reading and periodically visit the notes. One can read other books covering same or overlapping ideas after some time and reflect on the content.

Using the content

Strictly speaking, utilisation of knowledge is not part of effective reading. One can read fast, understand the content and retain the content without using it. But this will add no value to the reader. To properly utilise the content, one needs to spend time on the content which is required for him. There are few benefits of reading for the sake of reading itself.

  1. Reading more content makes you a better reader and improve your reading skill.
  2. It will make you a very knowledgable person. You can eventually find avenues to use your knowledge. You can connect with more people and ideas.
  3. Reading for pleasure is also a great hobby to relax.
